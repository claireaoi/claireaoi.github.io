Digestion 
Augmented Altered Artificial Archeology

If AI Collective  is a collective which attempts to open a conversation about how citizens wish to see smart new technologies affecting their lives, in order to enrich the familiar narratives around AI-infused tomorrows with local, situated and inclusive practices. It encompasses direct fieldwork, online conversations, playful experiments and workshops, as means to rethink the place and impact of that Artificial Intelligence could have within our society.

The project critically casts narrow visions of future as a recycled algorithmic version of the past.
Aiming at removing the creative element out of any human shell — while feeding a recurrent neural network with audio and midi from CTM artists — the digestion of this heteroclite sonic archive throws up a distorted atonal mash-up of the past.
It will embrace its strangeness, its non-intentionality, its atonality, and assume this future as a recycled digital algorithmic version of the past.
Art creation is broadcasted as an altered — and faulty — archeology.
